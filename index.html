Welcome

Tetris is a legendary tile-matching puzzle video game where the objective is to manipulate falling blocks, known as Tetriminos, to create complete rows without any gaps.

Created by Russian game designer Alexey Pajitnov in 1984, Tetris has become one of the most iconic and best-selling video games of all time.

The game's simple yet challenging gameplay has captivated players worldwide for decades. As rows are completed, they disappear, and the game speeds up, presenting an increasing challenge.

Join millions of players who have enjoyed the timeless fun and addictive nature of Tetris. Whether you're a beginner or a Tetris master, the excitement never ends!
